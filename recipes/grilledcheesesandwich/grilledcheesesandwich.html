Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

A Grilled Cheese Sandwich is a crispy, golden sandwich filled with gooey melted cheese. Itâ€™s a classic comfort food, quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook the sandwich until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with ketchup or soup.
