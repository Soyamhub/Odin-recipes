Fruit Salad

Description

Fruit Salad is a refreshing mix of seasonal fruits tossed with a hint of lemon juice and sometimes honey or mint for added flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup diced apples
  • 1 cup chopped bananas
  • 1/2 cup grapes
  • 1/2 cup diced mango or pineapple
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Optional: 1 tbsp honey or chopped mint

Steps

  1. Chop all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Mix them in a large bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice and honey (if using).
  4. Toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve fresh or chilled.
