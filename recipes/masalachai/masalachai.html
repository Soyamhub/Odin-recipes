Masala Chai

Description

Masala Chai is a traditional Indian tea flavored with aromatic spices and milk. It’s warm, comforting, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp black tea leaves
  • 2–3 cardamom pods
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 2–3 cloves
  • 1-inch ginger (crushed)
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water with all the spices and ginger.
  2. Simmer for 3–4 minutes to release flavors.
  3. Add tea leaves and boil for 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk and sugar, then bring to a boil again.
  5. Simmer for a minute and strain into cups.
