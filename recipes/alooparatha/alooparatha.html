Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular Indian stuffed flatbread filled with spiced mashed potatoes, pan-fried until golden brown. It's usually served with curd or pickle.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt, turmeric, and garam masala to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare a soft dough using flour and water.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with spices and herbs.
  3. Roll a small dough ball, place filling inside, and seal.
  4. Roll out into a flat circle gently.
  5. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee/oil until golden on both sides.
