Paneer Butter Masala

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian rice dish made by layering fragrant basmati rice with spiced meat (like chicken, mutton, or beef) or vegetables. It's cooked with herbs, saffron, and sometimes yogurt, creating a rich, savory blend of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken or mutton (or vegetables for veg biryani)
  • Onions (thinly sliced)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala or whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done.
  2. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it sit for 30–60 minutes.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, sauté whole spices, then add sliced onions and fry until golden brown.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Then add marinated meat and cook until tender and oil separates.
  5. In a large pot or handi, layer partially cooked rice over the cooked meat.
  6. Sprinkle chopped coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk (if using) on top.
  7. Cover tightly and cook on low flame (dum) for 20–25 minutes.
  8. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Mix gently and enjoy!

Description

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy North Indian curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a rich tomato-butter gravy.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt, red chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Sauté onions in butter until soft.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook briefly.
  3. Pour in tomato puree and spices, cook until oil separates.
  4. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and cook for another 2 minutes.
  6. Serve with naan or rice.
