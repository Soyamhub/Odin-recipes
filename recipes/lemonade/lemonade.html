Lemonade

Description

Lemonade is a cool and refreshing drink made with lemon juice, water, and sugar. It's perfect for hot days and easy to prepare at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons (juiced)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 2â€“3 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional: mint leaves

Steps

  1. Mix lemon juice and sugar in a jug.
  2. Add cold water and stir well until sugar dissolves.
  3. Add ice cubes and mint leaves (optional).
  4. Serve chilled.
