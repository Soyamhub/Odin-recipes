Three Bean Salad

Descrption

Three Bean Salad is a classic, refreshing side dish made with a mix of three types of beans—typically green beans, kidney beans, and garbanzo (chickpeas) or wax beans. The beans are tossed in a tangy-sweet dressing made from vinegar, oil, sugar, and seasonings. Often served chilled, it's a popular choice for picnics, potlucks, and barbecues due to its simplicity, bold flavor, and long shelf life.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green beans (trimmed and blanched)
  • 1 cup kidney beans (cooked or canned)
  • 1 cup garbanzo beans (chickpeas, cooked or canned)
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/3 cup vinegar (white or apple cider)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2–3 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Blanch green beans in boiling water for 2–3 minutes, then transfer to ice water. Drain and set aside.
  2. Rinse and drain kidney beans and garbanzo beans if using canned.
  3. In a large bowl, combine all three types of beans and sliced red onions.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together vinegar, olive oil, sugar, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  5. Pour the dressing over the beans and toss well to combine.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour to let the flavors meld.
  7. Stir before serving and garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
