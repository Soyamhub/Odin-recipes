Lasagna Recipe

Descrption

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with a rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and grated cheese like mozzarella or Parmesan. It's baked until bubbly and golden, resulting in a hearty and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs

Steps

  1. Sauté chopped onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato/marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles (if not oven-ready) until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. Mix ricotta cheese with salt, pepper, and an optional egg in a bowl.
  6. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the baking dish.
  7. Add a layer of noodles.
  8. Spread ricotta cheese mixture over noodles.
  9. Add a layer of meat sauce.
  10. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, cheese).
  12. Finish with a top layer of cheese.
  13. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25–30 minutes.
  14. Remove foil and bake for another 10–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  15. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
