Vegetable Stir Fry

Description

Vegetable Stir Fry is a quick and healthy dish made by sautéing a variety of vegetables in a savory sauce. It's perfect as a main or side dish with rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or sesame oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add all vegetables and stir fry for 4–5 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  5. Cook for another 2 minutes until veggies are tender-crisp.
  6. Serve hot with rice or noodles.
