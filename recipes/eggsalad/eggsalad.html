Egg Salad

Description

Egg Salad is a creamy and protein-rich dish made with hard-boiled eggs and a tangy mayo-based dressing. Itâ€™s great in sandwiches or as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-boiled eggs (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tbsp chopped celery
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped parsley or green onions

Steps

  1. Peel and chop the hard-boiled eggs.
  2. In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add eggs and chopped celery to the dressing.
  4. Mix gently to combine all ingredients.
  5. Chill before serving or use as sandwich filling.
